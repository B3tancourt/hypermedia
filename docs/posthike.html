Post Hike

So you just finished your hike, now what?

Photo by Nina Uhlíková from Pexels

Consider this checklist when you're done with your trip!

  • Check for ticks
  • Wash off your boots
  • Take care of any trash
  • Rehydrate, always!
  • Strech
  • Get a good meal (carbs, leafy greens)
  • Shower! Your spouse will thank you
