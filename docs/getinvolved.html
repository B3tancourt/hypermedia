Looking to get involved?

Did you know SUNY Oswego has their own outdoor club? Although there may be restrictions due to COVID, consider looking into this club in the future. This organization is the perfect way to get introduced to hiking with experienced people. Many of their trips include overnight stays, snowshowing, rockclimbing, and even whitewater rafting!
